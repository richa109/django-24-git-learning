    Expense management is a bookkeeping system for controlling, tracking, and processing employee expense reimbursements and employee-related corporate credit card transactions. Specialized online and mobile app expense management software integrates with accounting software and ERP systems.

    Expense management processes include:

    • Establishing company policies
    • Issuing corporate credit cards and virtual cards to employees for routine purchases
    • Having employees submit receipts
    • Controlling expenditures and the reporting process
    • Making payments to reimburse employees for approved expenses

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Some of th questions are given below to simplify your expense management process

    • What is expense management?

      Expense management is the process of tracking, reporting, and reimbursing employees for their business expenses per company policies and procedures. Now no employee wants to manually enter the price of every coffee or meal they had during a business trip, and no approver wants to spend hours manually verifying these disorganized reports. This is where an expense management system comes in, with the right software in place, your employees can report their expenses with ease and get reimbursed on time.

    • Why is expense management important?

      Expense management helps organizations control costs, ensure compliance, improve visibility into employee spending, and streamline the reimbursement process. By tracking and managing employee expenses, businesses can optimize spend, identify and prevent fraud, and make better financial decisions.

    • What are the benefits of expense management automation?

      Using an expense management software can help you in the following ways: Save time and eliminate mistakes: AI automatically processes receipts, eliminating manual data entry and speeding up reimbursements. Simplify compliance: Track spending effortlessly with built-in expense policies and audit trails, simplifying tax season. Empower employees: Offers a user-friendly mobile app, making expense reporting a breeze for your team. Gain insights: Track trends and optimize spending with powerful analytics and reporting tools.

    • What are real-time credit card feeds?

      Real-time credit card feeds is credit card transaction data received directly from card networks like Mastercard, Visa, or American Express without being dependent on the issuing bank. The data is received as soon as a business credit card is swiped and can hence be used to track and control spend, budgets, reconcile card transactions without manual effort, and identify fraudulent transactions.

    • What is an expense management software?

      Expense management software is the best alternative to manual expense management as it simplifies and automates the entire purchase-to-reimbursement process. With an expense management platform, businesses can automate expense report collection and credit card reconciliation, streamline expense approvals, integrate with top accounting software, and fasten reimbursements.
